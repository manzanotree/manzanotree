Create a budget for a day, week, month, or year, and see your spending update as payments are added.

**What you can do with taptap**
- Create budgets for flexible time periods
- Track tap payments automatically using Shortcuts
- Manually add or edit payments anytime
- View your current spend at a glance
- Get optional notifications as you spend
- Works fully offline

**Designed for privacy**

taptap does not connect to banks, cards, NFC, or payment networks.
All data stays on your device. No accounts. No cloud storage. No tracking.
Only the payment amount, merchant, and issuer are passed into the app via a user-configured Shortcut.

**Who it’s for**

taptap is ideal for anyone who wants lightweight budgeting without the complexity, noise, or privacy tradeoffs of traditional finance apps.
